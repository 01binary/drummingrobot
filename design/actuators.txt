Arm servo
	Mass to move: 8 lb (above) + 2.2 lb + 0.14lb * 2 + 0.083 * 2 = ~10.7 lbs
	I = 16221.970 lbmass in^2
	How fast = rad/s = .8rad (45deg) in 1 second
	Torque = I (lbs in^2) * a (radians/s^2)
	Torque = 16221.970 lb in^2 * .8 rad/s^2
	Torque = 16221.970 lb/in
	Torque = 1832.84 Nm
	Torque = 259,551.52 Oz/in
	Absolute min is 1622.197 (if moving .1 rad/sec)
	Absolute min in oz = 25955.153034 oz/in
